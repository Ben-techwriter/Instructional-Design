# Learning Objectives

After completing this module, the learner will be able to:

### Cognitive (Bloom’s Apply/Analyze)
- Explain the purpose of modular content in instructional design  
- Identify the appropriate authoring tool for different training needs  
- Apply storyboarding techniques to create a microlearning lesson  

### Psychomotor
- Demonstrate how to publish an HTML5 eLearning module  

### Affective
- Recognize the importance of accessibility in instructional materials
